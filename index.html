About Me

Hello there! My name is Steven Rejdukowski and I am a developer at Code Fellows and this is a little website about me. I currently reside in Chicago and have been in Illinois all my life. I went to the College of Dupage for a few years and received a certificate in music production. I am excited about learning and taking in as much information as I can during my Code Fellows experience as well as making new connections with fellow developers. Thanks for stopping by my page!
